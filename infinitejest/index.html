Infinite Jest: a scene-by-scene guide

By Professor Keith O'Neill

Note: This guide was not written by me. It was originally written and published by Keith O'Neill, Associate Professor of English and Humanities at SUNY-Dutchess. It was originally published on his universiy webpage (broken link). It was a resource that I found incredibly valuable both during and after my two reads of Infinite Jest

In June 2018, I attempted to find this guide, only to discover that it was no longer online. I was able to find an archived copy, and wanted to put it back online, so this amazing resource could continue to be used.

I attempted to contact Professor O'Neill to get his permission to publish his text, but I have not recieved a response. Professor O'Neill, if you're reading this and want me to remove this guide from my website, just let me know

Enjoy!

-Dann, June 2018
